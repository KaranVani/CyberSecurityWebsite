The Importance Of Naming Files

Published on April 5th, 2021 by Fin Watling

When working with computers, one of the first most basic things you should learn is that naming your files properly is really important! if you want to be able to find your work in a hurry, this will be much easier with a clear and concise naming convention. For example, if you are working on an essay, you may want to name the document the essay title, or if you’re working on a spreadsheet, you’ll want the title to give an overview of what the data represents.
